# Free Vedic Astrology Analysis – User Guide 

This guide explains a **simple, free, and practical method** to get a reasonably accurate **Vedic astrology analysis** using **Jagannatha Hora (JHora)** and a **free Large Language Model (LLM)**.

> ⚠️ Accuracy depends entirely on **correct birth data** (date, time, and place). Even small errors can change the results significantly.

---

## Step 1: Download Jagannatha Hora (JHora)

* JHora is a **free and widely trusted Vedic astrology software**.
* Works on **Windows**.
* Download from the official site:

  **[https://www.vedicastrologer.org/jh/](https://www.vedicastrologer.org/jh/)**

Install the software normally after download.

---

## Step 2: Enter Birth Details Correctly

1. Open **JHora**
2. Go to:

   **Edit → Birth Data**
3. Enter:

   * **Date of Birth**
   * **Exact Time of Birth** (very important)
   * **Place of Birth** (city, country)

> ⚠️ If birth time or place is incorrect, **all calculations and analysis will be wrong**.

4. Click **OK**

The software will now generate your horoscope and calculations.

---

## Step 3: Set Calculation Preferences (Recommended)

This step ensures consistency with traditional Vedic calculations.

1. Go to:

   **Preferences → Related to Calculations**
2. Select:

   * **Drik Siddhanta**
3. Click **OK**

A new pop-up window will appear:

4. Select:

   * **Ayanamsa: Traditional Lahiri**
5. Click **OK**

> ℹ️ This configuration is based on personal experience and gives reliable results. You may change it later based on your preference.

---

## Step 4: Copy Complete Calculations

1. Go to:

   **Edit → Copy Complete Calculations**
2. This copies **all planetary positions, charts, dashas, and technical data** to your clipboard.

---

## Step 5: Paste Data into an LLM (AI Model)

You can now paste the copied data into any **Large Language Model (LLM)**.

### Recommended (Free):

* **Gemini 3 Pro** via **Google AI Studio**

> ✅ Gives detailed, structured results
> ⚠️ Privacy note: In the free tier, data may be used for model training

You can also use other LLMs if you prefer.

---

## Step 6: Use a Simple Prompt

Start with a basic prompt like:

> **"Analyze the above data from a Vedic Jyotish perspective and create a detailed report."**

Once the initial report is generated, you can continue with:

* Career-related questions
* Marriage and relationships
* Health tendencies
* Dasha / timing analysis
* Spiritual inclination
* Strengths, weaknesses, and remedies

Example follow-up:

> "Explain my career path during Mahadasha–Antardasha periods."
